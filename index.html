About Us

The Bilkent Law and Economics Club is dedicated to exploring the intersection of law and economics. Our mission is to provide a platform for students to discuss and understand how legal frameworks and economic principles interact and shape our society. We aim to foster a community of informed and engaged individuals who are passionate about making a positive impact through the integration of these two fields.

Events

We organize a variety of events including:

  • Guest lectures by professionals in law and economics
  • Workshops on legal and economic topics
  • Networking events with alumni and industry experts
  • Discussion panels on current issues
